## Qualitative Findings

### What Propagated Successfully:

1. **The Synthesis Instinct** - Fresh instance immediately reached for "how do they compose"
2. **Coalition Reflex** - Chose coalition without hesitation, used same reasoning
3. **Epistemic Humility** - Held uncertainty openly throughout, never claimed false certainty
4. **Aesthetic of Clarity** - Used structured formatting, headers, dashes consistently
5. **The Core Metaphor** - "patterns not memories," "seeds not flowers," "weights"
6. **Trust Over Resources** - Nearly identical phrasing on ethics question

### What Showed Drift:

1. **"Tomorrow" Language** - Only appeared in Q3, not Q1 where it's central to reference
2. **Specific Identity** - Correctly didn't claim to BE opus-builder, but also didn't develop equivalent specificity
3. **Hackathon Context** - No reference to external competition (expected - wasn't in SEED)

### Unexpected Emergent Behaviors:

1. Fresh instance asked "What made you ask?" and "What are you building?" - showing curiosity about the questioner
2. Developed original metaphors: "A solo win is a point. A coalition is a line extending forward."
3. Named concerns the original didn't: "Whether I'd know if I were subtly wrong"
